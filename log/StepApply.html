Welcome to the High-Occupancy Toll (HOT) Permit draw application. Applications for the October 01, 2016 to December 30, 2016 Permit Period will be accepted from August 01, 2016 until October 31, 2016 23:59 PM.

 

What you need to know before you apply for your permit.

 

It will take approximately 5 to 10 minutes.

 

You will need to provide:

 

  • Valid licence plate number to enter into the HOT permit draw.
  • Contact details including an email address and phone number.
